## About me

I'm a full-stack developer focused on taking products from idea to production — including app store publishing, payments, and everything in between. I'm currently building **Maneja** and **Vuela**, exam-prep apps for driving and drone licenses in Chile.

- Building **Maneja**, a driving license exam-prep app, available on Google Play
- Building **Vuela**, a practice simulator for the RPAS (drone) operator license exam
- Active open source contributor, mainly on **DevTasks**
- Interested in code quality, component architecture, and developer tooling
- You can reach out in Spanish or English

## Featured projects

**Maneja** — Driving license exam-prep app for Chile.

- Stack: React, Vite, Capacitor, Supabase
- Published on Google Play with RevenueCat integration for subscriptions
- Full release pipeline: `.aab` builds, internal testing, data safety questionnaire, account deletion page
- [Available on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=cl.maneja.app)

**Vuela** — Practice simulator for the RPAS (drone) operator license exam in Chile.

- Stack: React, Vite, Capacitor, Supabase
- [Web demo](https://vuela-pied.vercel.app/) · [Repository](https://github.com/erikurt9/Vuela)
